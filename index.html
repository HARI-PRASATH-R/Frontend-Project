CRISTIANO RONALDO

Portuguese footballer

ACHIVEMENTS

Individual Awards: Ballon d'Or: 5 wins (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017). UEFA Champions League: Most goals (140), most assists (42), most appearances (183), and most goals in knockout rounds (67). FIFA World Cup: First player to score at five World Cups (2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, 2022). UEFA European Championship: Most goals (14), most assists (8). International Football: Most international goals (136), most international appearances (217). Titles with Clubs: Real Madrid: Four Champions League titles, two La Liga titles, two Copas del Rey, and two Spanish Super Cups. Manchester United: Five Premier League titles, one Champions League title, and one FA Cup. Juventus: Two Serie A titles, one Italian Cup. Other Notable Achievements: Guinness World Record: Most international wins in history (132). First player to score 10+ goals for three different clubs: Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Top scorer in major European leagues: He has won the European Golden Shoe multiple times. Led Portugal to their first major international title: UEFA Euro 2016.
